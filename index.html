I'm

Student, Singer, Conductor

I'm a College Student who loves music. I spend most of my time being a Singer and a Choir Conductor.

I was born in Bekasi on October 25, 2002. Currently, I am studying at Klabat University majoring in Information Systems, in my fifth semester.

I started singing at the age of 5 and joined a choir at the age of 8. Since then, I have been participating in children's choir competitions every year until the age of 17. Since the age of 15, I started being a choir conductor. At the age of 19, I joined the university choir and became a conductor. One of my achievements as a conductor was winning two gold medals at the Bali International Choir Festival in 2022.

I also play music instruments such as piano and violin since the age of 13
